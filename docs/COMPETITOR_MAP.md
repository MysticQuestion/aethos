# Aethos competitor / resource map

Market research for Western, Vedic/Jyotish, Human Design, numerology, BaZi/Chinese systems, I Ching, and astrocartography. Purpose: study the landscape so Aethos can integrate what others silo.

**Lane:** not more mystic content — better symbolic infrastructure.

| Lane | Examples | Strength | Weakness | Aethos play |

| Interpretation apps | Co–Star, CHANI, Sanctuary, The Pattern, Reha | Habit UX, psychology | Black-box math | High-level insight + **Show the Math** |
