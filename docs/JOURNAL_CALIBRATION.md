# Journal Calibration

Aethos supports an EMA-style calibration model.

Journal fields:

- mood
- stress
- focus
- sleep quality
- social connection
- conflict level
- creativity
- decision pressure
- body energy
- clarity
- free text
- tags
- linked timing window IDs

Calibration thresholds:

- fewer than 7 entries: insufficient data
- 7-20 entries: early learning
- 21-60 entries: emerging signal
- 60+ entries: stronger basis

Calibration language must remain observational. Aethos should say "pattern to observe," not "forecast."
